Any Questions?

What are our Covid-19 protocols?

We take the covid-19 pandemic extremley seriously. Our protocols and precaustions include:

  • vacuuming any and all soft surfaces
  • Fogging the area with CDC/EPA approved disinfectants
  • Using a Microfiber wipe with all linear surfaces
  • Fogging soft surfaces again
  • Disposing of any PPE, and decontamination
What is Considered a Bulk item?
  • Furniture Items
  • Bed Frames & Mattresses
  • Debris
  • Items that are too large for regular service containers
What is PPE?
Personal Protective Equipment is a standard that OSHA (Occupational Safety and Health Administration) requires to protect workers from workplace hazards. PPE can include safety glasses and shoes, earplugs or muffs, hard hats, respirators, or coveralls, vests and full body suits.
