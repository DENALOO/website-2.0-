About Grant's Cleaning Solutions

Grant's Cleaning Solutions is here to help you!

Grants Cleaning Solutions(GCS) is a black-owned cleaning company that provides service in Connecticut and Springfield areas. At GCS we specialize in Commercial and Residential cleaning, but our work is not limited to just that; we perform bulk removal services, … We provide exceptional service and are determined to give you the best possible so you can focus on the more important things in life. At Grants Cleaning Solutions, we understand that every business is unique. Not everybody has the same layout, which can create challenges. But here at GCS, we are more than happy to take on your request; whether it may be a one-time clean-out or a recurring service. Call for any questions or quotes today!

We believe that without their trust, our company would not have reached this level of success. That is why we are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality of service possible. We do this by observing the company values we have adhered to since day one.

Our Story

Trust, transparency, collaboration, commitment and caring. It all adds up to a culture focused on care – caring about our franchisees, our customers, and the communities our franchisees support. And it culminates with a steadfast commitment to giving back to those less fortunate through programs such as Homes for our Troops and Wounded Warriors.
